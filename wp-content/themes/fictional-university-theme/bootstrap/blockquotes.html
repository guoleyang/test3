Blockquotes

The blockquote element is used to present content from another source:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting

the future of

nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

From WWF's website

To show the quote on the right use the class .blockquote-reverse:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature.

The world's

leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

From WWF's website
